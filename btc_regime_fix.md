1. Break down contributing signals:

   * moving averages
   * price structure (HH/HL vs LH/LL)
   * volatility/trend strength metrics
   * timeframe contributions

2. Identify which signal(s) are triggering the uptrend classification

3. Verify multi-timeframe agreement:

   * check 1H, 4H, and 1D regime outputs
   * confirm whether classification is driven by only short-term data

   * strong uptrend
   * weak uptrend / recovery
   * misclassified due to insufficient constraints

5. Output:

   * exact reason for “uptrend” classification
   * missing conditions that would prevent this classification

Ensure regime classification reflects structural trend, not short-term price fluctuations.
